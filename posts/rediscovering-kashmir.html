A running list for rediscovering Kashmir's native culture

I grew up in exile with my Kashmiri Pandit family, inside the culture but with little sense of what life in the Valley was actually like. Most families don't talk about it; ours didn't either. This list is my attempt to find out through films and books. Only substantial work makes it: no propaganda, nothing that leans on the subject instead of the craft. If you're in the same boat, I hope it helps. I'll keep adding to it.
